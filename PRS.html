Welcome to my paper rock scissors game!

Welcome to my paper rock scissor game. This is a very small project to practice my coding skills. Most of the CSS attributes used are just for learning experience, and not for overall asthetic pleasure.

Directions: Click one of the three buttons (paper, rock, or scissors). This will be your choice. The computer will also make the same choice. Whoever wins will receive a point. The first player to reach 5 points will win the game.
